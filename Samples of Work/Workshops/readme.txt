This folder contains materials for the various workshop and professional developments I have given.

1. Intro to Python: Workbooks created to introduce students to python and aid them in preprocessing and designing text models.

2. Intro to R Shiny: Materials for a workshop introducing R Shiny and creating an app that replicates a page from fivethirtyeight.com

3. Inclusive presentations: A guide and resource list for fostering inclusivity in presentations.

4. Intro to R: Materials for students to learn foundational R skills.
